Frequently Asked Questions

How can pharmacies join the CTPharmLink NG network?

Pharmacies can join our network by filling out the partnership inquiry form on this page. Our team will reach out to provide information about the onboarding process, requirements, and benefits.

How does the medication verification system work?

Our medication verification system uses blockchain technology (VeChain) to track medications from manufacturer to patient. Each medication package has a unique QR code or verification code that can be scanned or entered into our platform to instantly confirm authenticity.

Which insurance providers are integrated with CTPharmLink NG?

We are currently integrated with several HMOs including Hygeia HMO, Avon HMO, Reliance HMO, and the National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS). We are continuously expanding our partnerships to include more insurance providers.

How can I report a counterfeit medication?

If you suspect a counterfeit medication, you can report it through our platform by using the verification feature. If a medication fails verification, you will be given the option to report it directly to the authorities. You can also contact our support team for assistance.
