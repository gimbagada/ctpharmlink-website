Medication Verification

Our blockchain-powered verification system uses VeChain technology to track medications from manufacturer to patient. Simply scan the QR code on your medication package or enter the verification code to instantly confirm authenticity.

Key Benefits

  • Complete medication history and origin information
  • Integration with NAFDAC's Mobile Authentication Service
  • Report counterfeit medications directly to authorities
