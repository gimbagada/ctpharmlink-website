About CTPharmLink NG

Our mission to revolutionize healthcare access in Nigeria

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To revolutionize access to safe, affordable, and insured medications for all Nigerians through an integrated technology-driven ecosystem.

Vision

A Nigeria where every citizen has access to authentic medications, comprehensive health insurance coverage, and personalized healthcare insights.

The Problem

Counterfeit Medications

40% of medications in Nigeria are counterfeit, resulting in over 100,000 deaths annually. Patients have no reliable way to verify the authenticity of their medications.

Low Insurance Penetration

95% of Nigerians pay out-of-pocket for healthcare, with only 5% covered by insurance. This leads to catastrophic healthcare expenses and limited access to essential medications.

Fragmented Supply Chains

Inefficient pharmaceutical supply chains lead to ₦10B in expired drugs wasted yearly. Medication shortages are common, especially in rural areas.

Slow Outbreak Responses

Delayed responses to disease outbreaks result in preventable spread and inadequate medication distribution to affected areas.

Our Solution

Blockchain Verification

Using VeChain technology to verify medication authenticity from manufacturer to patient. Every medication package can be verified with a simple scan.

  • End-to-end tracking from manufacturer to patient
  • QR code verification on medication packages
  • Integration with NAFDAC's Mobile Authentication Service
  • Counterfeit reporting system

Insurance Integration

Partnerships with HMOs and NHIS to increase insurance coverage and reduce out-of-pocket expenses for medications.

  • Real-time insurance coverage verification
  • Automated claims processing
  • Reduced waiting time for reimbursements
  • Transparent pricing and coverage information

AI-Driven Analytics

Predictive disease outbreak alerts and inventory management to optimize medication availability.

  • Disease outbreak prediction and alerts
  • Personalized health recommendations
  • Inventory optimization for pharmacies
  • Medication adherence reminders

Integrated Pharmacy Network

Connecting patients with verified pharmacies and efficient medication delivery services.

  • Nationwide network of verified pharmacies
  • Real-time medication availability information
  • Last-mile delivery through logistics partners
  • Rural access solutions
